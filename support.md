# Heavy Protocol Support

## Need Help?
If you are experiencing issues with the app, have a feature request, or just want to discuss Heavy Duty training principles, we are here to help.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**

A: A: All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app without a cloud backup (iCloud or Google Drive), your data will be lost. We recommend keeping device backups enabled in your system settings.

**Q: I found a bug. How do I report it?**

A: Please email us with the details below. If the issue is visual (e.g., charts looking wrong), a screenshot is incredibly helpful!
